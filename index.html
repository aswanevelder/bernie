ABOUT BERNIE WEEDEN

Born in Zimbabwe and now resident in South Africa, Bernie Weedenâ€™s artistic skills are well known by many. If there is a creative skill in the artistic arena, Bernie has more than likely attempted it. She is the author of a number of books, paints beautifully, enjoys woodwork, and loved her limited experience with pottery. Her gifts are diverse and her work captivating. Whilst she uses various art mediums, more recently she has focused on pastel and graphite pencil art work. Some of her originals - and canvas prints - are available for sale and can been seen on this web site.

Her love for art was evident in her early years, growing up in Zimbabwe. She is a self-taught artist with no formal art training. Much of her life has been in the wild outdoors and her eye for detail and a love for creating has resulted in her capturing and portraying life in the subjects she paints. Bernie is inspired and absorbed by all she sees and is motivated to record this in her work. Her love for nature, and the joy of creation is seen in what she does. Bernie is also a qualified picture framer, and is able to carefully select the frame and mountboard to ensure the painting is perfectly presented. Bernie and her husband, Ian, now live in Nelspruit, South Africa and they continue to explore the outdoors and travel into the Kruger National park and the Lowveld farms in their love for the bushveld.
